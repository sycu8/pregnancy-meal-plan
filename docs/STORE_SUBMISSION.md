### 2.2 English

```
Bầu Ăn Gì? helps expecting parents plan practical, Vietnamese home-style meals week by week.

The app generates a free 7-day meal plan based on gestational week, weight, taste preferences, budget and common concerns such as nausea, constipation, anemia, gestational diabetes or high blood pressure.

KEY FEATURES
• Free 7-day meal plans — no sign-in required
• Familiar Vietnamese dishes with reference portions
• Grouped grocery shopping lists
• Estimated meal and shopping-trip costs
• Pregnancy food-safety reminders
• Guidance for protein, iron, calcium, folate, omega-3, fiber and hydration
• Reference BMI and pregnancy weight-gain ranges
• Save profile and meal-plan history on your device
• Prenatal nutrition blog (Vietnamese and English)
• Vietnamese and English UI

WHO IT IS FOR
Pregnant women and families who want everyday meal ideas and a quick shopping list before going to the market.

TRUSTED REFERENCES
Nutrition and food-safety content references public guidance from ACOG, CDC, NHS, WHO and reputable Vietnamese hospitals.

IMPORTANT — NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE
Meal plans are generated automatically from the information you provide and are for reference only. The app does not diagnose, treat or replace advice from an obstetrician or registered dietitian. If you have gestational diabetes, hypertension, preeclampsia risk, severe anemia, fetal growth concerns or any medical issue, ask your clinician before following the plan.

PRIVACY
The free tier stores your profile and meal plans on your device. We do not sell personal data. Optional sync stores only fields needed for meal planning. Details: https://mebauangi.info/en/privacy

FREE DURING EARLY RELEASE
The app is free during the initial release. Premium features may be added later.

Support: support@mebauangi.info
```
